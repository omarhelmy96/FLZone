About Us

Who Are we?

ITI - as its initiative to support freelancers in the technical fields- announces the availability of freelancing zones on its branches (You can find ITI's branches locations on our website here: http://www.iti.gov.eg) that provides free work spaces with internet connection including coaching and consultation services for new graduates and junior freelancers.




Our Services

• Shared Work-space for freelancers (ITI’s graduates and external freelancers), and for graduates working remotely as full-time developers for local or global clients.

• Daily free discussion hour, for ideas sharing and business relations between expert and junior freelancers in the zone, and interaction among freelancers in work time is very limited to not affect senior freelancers’ time and work.
